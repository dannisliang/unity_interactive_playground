----Gestures---- 
https://developer.leapmotion.com/documentation/csharp/api/Leap.Gesture.html#id1
Hand Swipe -> Detects hand swiping on x and y axis. Can be tuned with MinLength and MinVelocity settings
Hand Swipe  Rotate -> Detects hand swipe on x and y axis then rotates game object
Hand Circle -> Detects  circle motion from fingers. Tuning via MinRadius and MinArc
